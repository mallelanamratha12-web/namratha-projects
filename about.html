About Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a famous German luxury automobile brand known for its high-quality cars, advanced technology, and elegant design. The brand is a symbol of comfort, safety, and performance.

Mercedes-Benz was founded in 1926 and is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
